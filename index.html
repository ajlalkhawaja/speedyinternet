Frequently Asked Questions

FAQs

Find answers to the most common questions about our internet services.

What internet speeds are available?

We offer a wide range of internet speeds to fit every household and business need. Plans start from reliable entry-level speeds for browsing and email, all the way up to gigabit internet for seamless streaming, gaming, and work-from-home performance. Availability may vary by location.

Can I add a phone line?

Yes! You can bundle your internet service with a home phone line for one affordable monthly price. Get unlimited local and nationwide calling, plus popular calling features like voicemail, call waiting, and caller ID.

How long does installation take?

Most standard installations are completed within a few hours. A professional technician will ensure that your equipment is properly set up and your connection is running smoothly. Same-day or next-day installation may also be available depending on your area.

What if I want to cancel?

You can cancel your service at any time. If you are on a promotional or contract plan, early termination fees may apply. We recommend contacting our customer care team to review your account details and discuss the best cancellation options available.
